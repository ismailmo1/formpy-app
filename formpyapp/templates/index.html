Get Started

It's not always possible to completely digitise forms, sometimes you need to stick to the tried and tested paper based forms for various reasons. Let's stop pushing digital to force a solution for your unique problem, instead automate the most time consuming part of forms: reading and analysing the data.

With formpy, you can pick a pre-designed template, or completely design your own from scratch. Print and distribute these forms as you would normally, upload the filled in forms and we will take it from there: reading, analysing and presenting the data in a format that suits your needs.
